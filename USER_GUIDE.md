## üöÄ What on earth is this?

Atlas_Core is a **technical prompt generator** (instructions for generative AIs).
It is used to create **Maps, Scenarios, Characters, and User Interfaces (UI)** for video games, RPGs, or novels.

**It does not generate images directly** (we don't do black magic yet), it generates the *perfect text* for you to paste into Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, or Gemini so they can do the dirty work.

## üéõÔ∏è The Three Modes of Happiness

The app has three personalities, depending on how much you want to work today:

### 1. üèóÔ∏è THE CONSTRUCTOR (Mode "I'm in a Rush")
Ideal for when you need inspiration **NOW** or feel too lazy to think.

*   **What does it do?** Offers predefined and curated prototypes.
*   **The Magic Button:** There is a button that says **"GENERATE NEW PROTOTYPES"** (or shuffle icon). Press it. The system will mix civilizations, places, and art styles randomly but coherently (or not, sometimes you get "Cyberpunk Ancient Egypt" and it rocks).
*   **Usage:** See a card you like -> Click -> Prompt copied -> Go generate.

### 2. üìê THE ARCHITECT (Mode "Control Freak")
For when you have an exact vision in your head and no one is going to tell you how to design your dungeon.

*   **Step by Step:** A wizard guides you through 10 steps. Scale, Theme, Civilization, Weather, Camera...
*   **Visual DNA:** This is where the magic happens. You define the exact style (Zelda? Dark Souls? Wes Anderson?).
*   **üì∏ New Feature - Style Theft (Oops, sorry, "Extraction"):**
    Do you have an image with a killer style and want to copy it? In step 6, upload that image. Atlas_Core will use its bionic eyes (Gemini Vision) to technically analyze the style and apply it to your prompt. Easy peasy.

### 3. üîÆ STORYCRAFTER (Mode "I want to make a whole game")
This is the heavyweight. Designed to create **coherent collections**. Because it's useless to have a "Pixel Art" map and an "Italian Renaissance" style character.

*   **The Flow:**
    1.  Choose a Race and a Place (or let chance decide).
    2.  Atlas_Core invents the context.
    3.  **Generate POIs:** Press the button and the AI will invent 6 logical places inside your building (e.g., if it's a Spaceship, it will suggest "Engine Room", "Command Bridge", etc.).
*   **The 3 Power Buttons:**
    *   üåç **World:** Generates tactical map, isometric views, and the 6 interiors.
    *   üîÆ **UI:** Generates buttons, dialog windows, and health bars that match your civilization.
    *   ‚öîÔ∏è **Characters:** Generates the Hero, Villain, NPCs, Pet, and even a sheet of badges (tokens) with their faces.
*   **Result:** A huge list of prompts that share the same visual DNA. Copy everything, paste it into your generator, and go to sleep.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Technical Configuration (The Fine Print)

In step 10 (or at the bottom), you choose the AI dialect:

1.  **Universal:** Rich natural language. Works well in DALL-E 3 and Gemini.
2.  **Midjourney:** The gold standard. Adds weird parameters like `--ar 16:9 --stylize 250`. If you use MJ, use this.
3.  **Technical (SD):** Just keywords separated by commas. For the brave souls using Stable Diffusion or ComfyUI.
